Alexandru Balan - README.txt
Acest fisier contine datele/codul susrsa/ alte 
componente care nu imi apartin in pearOS NiceC0re.
--------------------------------------------------

Bazat pe: Arch Linux (https://archlinux.org/)
Kernel(nucleu): Linux (https://www.kernel.org/) (prestabilit in Arch Linux)
Desktop Environment: KDE Plasma (https://github.com/KDE)
Manager Fereastra: K Window Manager (KWIN) (https://github.com/KDE/kwin) (Prestabilit in KDE Plasma)
Server de display: x.org (https://github.com/freedesktop/xorg-xserver) (prestabilit in KDE Plasma)
Bara de activitati si bara de meniu: Latte Dock ( cu configurari particularizate) (https://github.com/KDE/latte-dock)
Creearea imaginii ISO: archiso cu modificari ( de la linia 1393 pana la linia 1505 (112 linii)) (https://github.com/pearOS-archlinux/iso/blob/main/build-binary)
Iconita de la selectorul de teme: Realizata de Andrei Muntean (https://github.com/pearOS-archlinux/theme-switcher)
Pachetul de iconite: Bubble Icon pack (https://github.com/Pear-Project/pearOS-Default-Icons)
Tema de Lockscreen: bazata pe WhiteSur SDDM, cu modificari (https://github.com/Pear-Project/pearOS-Default-SDDM)
Tema de sistem: Autor: Eliver Lara, cu modificari (https://github.com/Pear-Project/pearOS-Default-Kvantum)
Tema de fereastra: bazata pe Sweet Mars aurorae theme (https://github.com/EliverLara/Sweet/tree/mars/kde/aurorae)
Tema de bootloader: Bazata pe SLEEK (https://github.com/sandesh236/sleek--themes)
Bootloader: GRUB2 (Este cel prestabilit in ArchLinux)
Website: Andrei Muntean a aujtat la structura(bara de navigatie se integreza bine in compozitia website-ului) ( https://pearos.xyz; https://nicec0re.pearos.xyz)
Bash sript-ul care instaleaza sistemul: m-am inspirat de la https://github.com/jorgeluiscarrillo/arch-setup, sriptul meu fiind disponibil pe https://github.com/pearOS-archlinux/pearOS-installer/blob/main/system_install/setup
Structura HTML de unde m-am inspirat pentru UI-ul instalatorului de sistem: https://github.com/zhovner/magic-setup-assistant (Precizari: sub 20% din original a fost utilizat in creearea pachetului meu)
Pentru a numara cate descarcari are pearOS, m-am folosit de GitHub API, iar pentru inspiratie am folosit  https://github.com/Somsubhra/github-release-stats
Pentru a afisa un cerc care creste(in https://nicec0re.pearos.xyz/get/) am folosit metodele de sinus si cosinus gasite pe https://stackoverflow.com/questions/5736398/how-to-calculate-the-svg-path-for-an-arc-of-a-circle si https://stackoverflow.com/questions/10756313/javascript-jquery-map-a-range-of-numbers-to-another-range-of-numbers
Pentru meniul din stanga sus, am luat majoritatea elemntelor de la https://github.com/kppleos/org.kpple.kppleMenu
Ca manager de fisiere: nautilus https://github.com/GNOME/nautilus
